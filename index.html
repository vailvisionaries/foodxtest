About Us

Food X Inspections LLC, or FXI for short was recently founded by Gene Gonzalez. While the company name may be new to the industry, its Founder is not. Gene offers 25 years’ experience in the X-Ray field with a specific focus in Real Time Food and Commodities Inspection. His knowledge, training and experience gives him a unique perspective into his customer’s needs and a great understanding of the importance of maintaining the products integrity. He recognizes every incident is unique and customizes a recovery plan to quickly return a safe, marketable product. In his many years he has evaluated and inspected a variety of products of all shapes, sizes and packaging. Strict confidentiality is always a priority for Gene and his team. FXI is committed to staying informed and educated on the latest industry standards and are always looking for ways to provide clients with the best experience possible.

We are located inside a 120,000 square foot cold storage facility with the capability to handle dry, refrigerated and frozen product. This facility is USDA/FSIS approved and maintained, FDA registered and AIB Certified.

INSPECTION SERVICES

fOOD X INSPECTIONS

The last thing any product manufacturer wants, especially in the Food Industry, is for a foreign contaminate to enter their product during processing and make its way to the consumer. Even with every in-house precaution and lines of defense in place, it still happens. When it does, disposal is not your only option. FXI offers inspection services using next generation technology to locate contaminates including glass, metal, bone, or stone. Let us help you recapture potentially lost profits with our fast and reliable inspection services.
Our custom built conveyorized X-Ray inspection equipment uses modern technology and was designed to accommodate product in a variety of packaging. Our digital imaging based system provides high image quality X-ray inspection to locate contaminates as small as .8mm, the USDA standard, for detection of ferrous metal in most food products.  So regardless of the issue you are faced with, contacting FXI for a free, confidential product evaluation could be the solution you are looking for.
No matter what product you manufacture, you can rely on FXI to provide you with an honest evaluation and recovery plan. Whether its food and beverage, pharmaceuticals, cosmetic or personal care, footwear, pet food and more, we can help. When your reputation depends on it, you can depend on FXI.
